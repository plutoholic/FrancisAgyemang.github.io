About Me

I’m a data analyst who loves transforming data into actionable insights. I specialize in SQL, Excel, Python, and data visualization, building projects that turn complex data into clear stories that drive decisions.

This portfolio showcases my work across Data Analytics, Graphic Design, and UX/UI Design. With a background that combines analytical thinking and creative problem-solving, I focus on building solutions that are both data-driven and visually engaging. In my data analytics projects, I work with tools such as SQL, Excel, Python, and Tableau to clean, explore, and analyze data, transforming complex datasets into meaningful insights and interactive visualizations that support informed decision-making.
Alongside analytics, I have experience in graphic design and UX/UI design, where I focus on creating intuitive user experiences and visually compelling designs. My design work emphasizes clarity, usability, and storytelling, ensuring that digital products and visual content communicate effectively with users. This portfolio highlights projects that demonstrate my ability to combine design and data, from data exploration and dashboards to user-centered digital experiences.
